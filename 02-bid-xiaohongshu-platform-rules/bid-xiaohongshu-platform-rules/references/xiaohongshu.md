# Xiaohongshu Publishing Rules

Updated: 2026-06-30

Scope: Xiaohongshu/RED China App and Creator Center. Backend upload limits are often shown inside the logged-in editor; verify before final delivery.

## Recommended Specs

| Type | Practical Export | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Image note cover | 1080x1440 (3:4) or 1080x1350 (4:5) | 3:4 is the common Xiaohongshu visual baseline; keep title in the center safe area. |
| Square image | 1080x1080 | Useful for reusable brand templates and cross-posting. |
| Vertical video | 1080x1920 (9:16) | Keep subtitles large and away from bottom UI. |
| Horizontal video | 1920x1080 (16:9) | Use only when the content itself is horizontal. |
| Carousel | Keep all images in the same ratio | Avoid mixing long images and square images in one note unless intentional. |

## Content and Compliance Checks

- Notes should be authentic and useful. Avoid fake experience, fabricated results, fake comparison, false scarcity, and exaggerated transformation.
- Marketing content should not disguise ads as personal experience when there is a commercial relationship.
- Sensitive verticals need stricter review: medical beauty, health products, finance, education, recruitment, legal, parenting, minors, cosmetics and food claims.
- Avoid off-platform diversion: QR codes, phone numbers, private chat bait, WeChat ID, transaction guidance, or wording that pushes users away from the platform.
- Avoid unauthorized portraits, product photos, brand marks, screenshots, fonts, music, and copied text or images.
- AIGC or synthetic people/voice/scene content should be disclosed when it can mislead users about reality.

## Title and Cover Guidance

- Make the cover readable on mobile. Avoid stuffing many small words into the first image.
- Avoid absolute words and guarantee-style claims such as "must", "100%", "permanent", "best", "cure", "risk-free".
- Prefer concrete scene, audience, result, and limitation. Example: "newcomer checklist" is safer than "the only correct method".

## Backend Verification Items

Verify in the live editor before final delivery:

- current max image count per note
- current single image size and supported formats
- current video size, duration, codec, and cover rules
- title length and hashtag behavior
- whether the account has brand/cooperation or store features enabled
