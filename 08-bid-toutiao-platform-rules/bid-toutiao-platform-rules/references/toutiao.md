# Toutiao Publishing Rules

Updated: 2026-06-30

Scope: Toutiao China, Toutiaohao creator platform, articles, micro-posts, videos, small videos, Q&A, columns, paid content, and content-commerce. Backend upload limits may vary by account capability and editor version; verify live before final delivery.

## Recommended Specs

| Type | Practical Export | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Article cover | 1280x720 or 900x383 variants | Prepare both news-feed wide cover and article-share crop when possible. Keep headline readable and avoid edge text. |
| In-article image | 900-1200 px width | Compress for mobile loading; cite source or ensure authorization. |
| Vertical video / small video | 1080x1920, mp4 | Use for mobile-first short videos and small-video style distribution. |
| Horizontal video | 1920x1080, mp4 | Use for explainers, interviews, news-style and course content. |
| Micro-post image | 1080x1080, 1080x1350, or 1080x1440 | Keep text large; avoid screenshots with private information. |
| Column/paid content cover | Verify in backend | Paid or column products can have separate cover and preview rules. |

## Publishing Routes

- **Toutiaohao article**: long-form article, news commentary, knowledge article, list article, tutorial, review.
- **Micro-post**: short text plus images, opinion, quick update, event comment, engagement post.
- **Video**: horizontal or vertical video submitted through creator backend.
- **Small video**: vertical mobile video route; verify current editor and account capability.
- **Q&A**: answer format should directly address the question and avoid irrelevant promotion.
- **Column / paid content**: verify account permission, preview behavior, title, cover, refund, and paid-content rules.
- **Content-commerce**: product card or affiliate content; check advertising, product claim, price, and transaction rules.

## Article and Title Checks

- Title should accurately match the content. Avoid false suspense, fake breaking news, exaggerated conflict, misleading celebrity/public-event wording, and title-body mismatch.
- For news-like or current-affairs content, cite reliable sources and avoid unverified casualties, identities, locations, policy claims, accusations, or screenshots.
- For knowledge and tutorial content, distinguish fact, interpretation, and personal judgment.
- Reprinted, compiled, translated, or summarized content should respect authorization, source citation, and originality rules.
- Avoid low-quality aggregation, article washing, machine-spun text, copied images, or screenshots without context.

## Video and Cover Checks

- Use a readable cover with accurate topic, no fake official style, no bait text, no misleading before-after comparison.
- Do not use unauthorized video clips, music, sports/event footage, TV/movie/anime clips, course recordings, paid materials, or platform watermarks.
- Subtitles should not cover important visual information or imply unverified facts.
- Verify video size, duration, codec, resolution, frame rate, and cover crop in the Toutiaohao backend before delivery.

## Commerce and Sensitive Verticals

Extra caution for:

- medical, health products, supplements, cosmetics, food claims
- finance, investment, insurance, loans, wealth management
- education, training, exams, certificates, recruitment, side jobs
- legal, public security, public events, disaster, charity
- minors, parenting, emotion, marriage, body image

For content-commerce:

- Avoid unverified efficacy claims and absolute words.
- Do not imply guaranteed income, guaranteed result, official endorsement, or limited-time scarcity without evidence.
- Disclose commercial cooperation or affiliate nature where applicable.
- Avoid private transaction guidance, contact harvesting, QR-code diversion, and off-platform deal bait.

## AIGC

- Label AI-generated or synthetic content when it may confuse users about real people, real events, product effects, evidence, voice, image, or scene.
- Do not create fake news, fabricated screenshots, fake expert statements, false public-event evidence, or impersonation content.
- The publisher remains responsible for AI output, including copyright, portrait, reputation, and misinformation risks.

## Backend Verification Items

Verify in Toutiaohao before final delivery:

- article title length, cover crop, image count, supported formats
- video upload size, duration, codec, frame rate, resolution, and cover requirements
- micro-post image count and GIF/video support
- Q&A, column, paid content, product card, and commerce permissions
- originality, reprint, citation, and account vertical restrictions
