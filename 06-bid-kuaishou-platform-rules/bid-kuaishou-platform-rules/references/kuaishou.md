# Kuaishou Publishing Rules

Updated: 2026-06-30

Scope: Kuaishou App, creator services, Kuaishou live, and Kuaishou e-commerce content.

## Recommended Specs

| Type | Practical Export | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Vertical video | 1080x1920 (9:16), mp4 | Best default for Kuaishou feed. |
| Horizontal video | 1920x1080 (16:9), mp4 | Use when source is landscape. |
| Cover | 1080x1920 or 1080x1440 | Keep title and main subject in center safe area. |
| Image post | 1080x1440, 1080x1350, or 1080x1080 | Keep carousel ratios consistent. |
| Live cover | Verify in live creation flow | Live and e-commerce live may show different requirements. |

## Compliance Checks

- Do not use false, exaggerated, or unverifiable product claims.
- Avoid private transaction guidance, QR-code diversion, contact harvesting, and off-platform bait.
- E-commerce content must follow product category, quality, price, after-sale, and advertising rules.
- Live content includes cover, title, host speech, product display, background, guests, comments, and transaction guidance.
- Respect portrait, trademark, music, footage, font, screenshot, and product-photo rights.
- AIGC content that may mislead users about reality should be labeled.

## Sensitive Areas

Extra review caution: medical, health products, finance, education, recruitment, legal, cosmetics, food, agricultural products, minors, public events, charity, and disaster content.

## Backend Verification Items

Verify before final delivery:

- current upload size, duration, codec, and image count
- cover crop behavior and title length
- live cover and live-room restrictions
- e-commerce category rules and prohibited words
- account permissions for links, shop, local life, or paid promotion
