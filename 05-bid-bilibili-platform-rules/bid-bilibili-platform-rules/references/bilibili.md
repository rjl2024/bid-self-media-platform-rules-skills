# Bilibili Publishing Rules

Updated: 2026-06-30

Scope: Bilibili creator center, video submission, article/dynamic content, and community rules.

## Recommended Specs

| Type | Practical Export | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Horizontal video | 1920x1080 (16:9), mp4 | Best default for Bilibili videos, tutorials, reviews, and explainers. |
| Vertical video | 1080x1920 (9:16), mp4 | Use for vertical source or mobile-first content. |
| Cover | 1920x1080 or 1280x720 | Keep text readable and avoid misleading clickbait. |
| Subtitle | SRT/ASS or burned-in subtitle depending on workflow | Keep subtitles consistent and readable. |
| Article image | 900-1200 px width | Use compressed web images for loading speed. |

## Metadata Guidance

- Choose the correct partition/category; wrong category can reduce distribution or trigger review.
- Title should describe the content honestly. Avoid misleading title bait, fake official tone, fake controversy, or fabricated conflict.
- Tags should match actual topic, character, product, game, software, or knowledge area.
- Description should disclose sources, references, sponsorship, AIGC involvement, and material authorization when relevant.

## Compliance Checks

- Respect copyright for video clips, anime/game footage, music, fonts, screenshots, cover art, and third-party logos.
- Avoid piracy, paid-course leakage, unauthorized recording, reposted videos, and watermark removal.
- AIGC content that may confuse users about real people, events, voice, or evidence should be labeled.
- Sensitive claims in finance, medical, legal, education, product efficacy, minors, and public events require extra caution.

## Backend Verification Items

Verify in Bilibili creator backend before final delivery:

- current upload size and duration limit
- supported codecs, resolution, frame-rate, and HDR options
- cover size and crop behavior
- title, tag, category, subtitle, and interactive-video capabilities
- live and commercial content rules if applicable
