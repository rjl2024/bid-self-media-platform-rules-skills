# Weibo Publishing Rules

Updated: 2026-06-30

Scope: Weibo posts, image/video publishing, long posts, topics, super topics, and commercial content.

## Recommended Specs

| Type | Practical Export | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Single image | 1080x1440, 1080x1350, or 1080x1080 | Use clear mobile-readable text. |
| Multi-image grid | 1080x1080 or consistent 4:5/3:4 | Grid previews crop differently across clients. |
| Vertical video | 1080x1920 (9:16), mp4 | Good for mobile feed. |
| Horizontal video | 1920x1080 (16:9), mp4 | Good for interviews, news, explainers. |
| Long post cover | 1080x1440 or 1080x1350 | Keep headline concise and centered. |

## Text and Topic Guidance

- Use hashtags only when they match the content. Avoid topic hijacking, event-riding, rumor amplification, and fake public-interest framing.
- Avoid fake official tone, fabricated screenshots, fake breaking news, undisclosed ads, and exaggerated product claims.
- For public events, cite reliable sources and avoid unverified identity, location, casualty, policy, or accusation claims.
- For commercial campaigns, disclose commercial cooperation where needed and avoid disguised advertising.

## Compliance Checks

- Respect portrait, privacy, reputation, trademark, music, video clip, screenshot, and image copyright.
- Avoid cyberbullying, doxxing, hate speech, vulgarity, illegal sales, gambling, fraud, and personal-data leakage.
- AIGC content that may confuse users about real people, evidence, news, or public events should be labeled.
- Sensitive verticals: medical, finance, legal, education, recruitment, minors, public events, charity, health products, cosmetics, food.

## Backend Verification Items

Verify in Weibo backend before final delivery:

- current video size, duration, codec, and cover requirements
- image count, size, and GIF support
- long post editor behavior
- topic/super-topic and commercial campaign requirements
- account permissions for links, paid promotion, live, or shop features
