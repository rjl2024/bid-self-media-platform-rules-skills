# WeChat Channels Publishing Rules

Updated: 2026-06-30

Scope: WeChat Channels China, Channels Assistant, and WeChat ecosystem content. Public stable media limits are less complete than backend editor prompts; verify in the account before final upload.

## Recommended Specs

| Type | Practical Export | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Vertical video | 1080x1920 (9:16), mp4 | Best default for Channels feed. Keep subtitles out of bottom UI. |
| Horizontal video | 1920x1080 (16:9), mp4 | Use for courses, interviews, screen recordings, and landscape source material. |
| Video cover | 1080x1440 (3:4) or 1080x1920 (9:16) | Keep title and face in center safe area. |
| Image post | 1080x1440, 1080x1350, or 1080x1080 | Keep text readable in WeChat mobile preview. |
| Live cover | Verify in live creation flow | Live entry may show current ratio and size requirements. |

## Compliance Checks

- Avoid off-platform traffic diversion, private transaction bait, QR code abuse, and contact harvesting.
- Product or service claims should be truthful and supported. Health, finance, education, recruitment, and legal claims need stricter review.
- Do not use unauthorized music, clips, screenshots, portraits, trademarks, or third-party logos.
- AIGC content that could mislead users about real people, real events, or product effects should be labeled.
- Live content includes title, cover, host speech, background, guests, comments, products, and links.

## Backend Verification Items

Verify in Channels Assistant before final delivery:

- current max video size and duration
- supported video formats and codec constraints
- current image count and image size
- cover ratio and title length
- product link, mini-program, live, paid content, and shop rules for the account
