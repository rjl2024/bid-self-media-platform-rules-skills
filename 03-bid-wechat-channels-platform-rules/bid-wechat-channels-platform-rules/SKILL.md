# WeChat Channels Platform Rules

Use this skill for WeChat Channels content. Many operational limits are exposed inside Channels Assistant or the mobile editor, so verify account-specific limits before final delivery.

## Workflow

1. Identify content type: video, image post, live, live cover, product-linked content, account profile asset.
2. Read `references/wechat-channels.md`.
3. Check WeChat ecosystem restrictions: external diversion, private transaction guidance, mini-program/product links, account identity, and live rules.
4. Flag uncertain upload limits as "verify in Channels Assistant".

## Response Pattern

Give recommended export settings, compliance risks, and backend-verification items. For commercial content, include product-claim and rights checks.
